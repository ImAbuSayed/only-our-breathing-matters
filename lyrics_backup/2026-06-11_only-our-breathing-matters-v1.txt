[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The whole world fades when you pull me near
There’s nothing left but the truth right here
Your skin on mine in the fading light
We’re stopping time on a restless night

[Verse 1]
We left the noise of the town behind
To see the stars that the shadows hide
The gentle breeze and the open road
Are taking us where we want to go

[Pre-Chorus]
And the closer we get, the slower we move
I’m losing my breath in the rhythm of you
The silence is deep, but the feeling is right
I’m crossing the line in the heat of the night

[Chorus]
When only our breathing matters
And every other sound just shatters
I want to pull you close and take my time
To feel the spark when your hands find mine

[Post-Chorus]
Only our breathing, only our breathing
I’m never leaving, no, I’m never leaving

[Verse 2]
Your heavy eyes looking up at me
You pull me in like the midnight sea
I trace a line on your shoulder blade
A silent promise that will never fade

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
Only our breathing, only our breathing

[Bridge]
The shadows dance across the wooden floor
I never knew what I was searching for
A whispered truth is echoing so deep
This is a promise that I want to keep
When every other vision fades to gray
The fire in Saeed will light the way

[Spoken]
Just close your eyes and let the world fade out.
It is only you and me right now.

[Final Chorus]
